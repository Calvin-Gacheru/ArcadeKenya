Game Rules

Welcome to "254Chronicles"! The goal of the game is for one player to explain a word to their teammates without saying the word itself.

  1. Teams: A Minimum of 2 teams is required to play.
  2. Translation: A player is not allowed to use a direct translation of the word. If a player uses a direct translation, the word will be considered incorrect.
  3. Explainer: One player from a team is the explainer for their round.
  4. The Card: A card will display a single word.
  5. The Challenge: The explainer must get their teammates to guess the word without saying the word itself.
  6. Round Length: Each team's turn consists of 5 words.
  7. Timer: You have a total of 30 seconds for the entire round.
  8. Scoring:
    • 1 point for each correctly guessed word (press "Next").
    • No penalty for skipping (press "Skip").
    • If a player skips a word but still has time after the round, they can attempt to guess the skipped word(s) for a point by reviewing the words after the round ends.
  9. Buttons:
    • Next: Tap this when your team guesses the word correctly, and to move to the next word.
    • Skip: Tap this if you want to pass on the current word and move to the next.
    • Pause/Resume: Pause or resume the game timer.
  10. Winning: The team with the highest total score after all teams have played their selected number of rounds wins!

Have fun and test your Kenyan knowledge!
